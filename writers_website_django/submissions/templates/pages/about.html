This website was created to create a community of readers and writers.

There is no pay wall, no names attached, and a matching system that allows writers to recieve feedback from readers interested in their work.

It was created as a part of a senior keystone project for Kilichand Honors College at Boston University by myself, Libby James.

I have always loved writing and reading, and I wanted to create a community for those who felt the same way. I wanted to give writers access to readers who are in their target audience, and I wanted to give readers access to stories and writers that are in their wheelhouse, so I created this website to match readers and writers to find stories they love. I also wanted to make it anonymous, as I researched the benefits of anonymous feedback, and they seem wonderful. Additionally, I hope it helps those who may be more timid when it comes to putting their write out into the world.

I hope that one day, this website may lead to more writers putting their work out into the world.
