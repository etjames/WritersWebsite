Looking for some writing inspiration? Here are 10 fun prompts!

1. Write a story that takes place in 2 consecutive subway cars.
2. Write a poem describing an object without ever mentioning the object's name.
3. Write a script where no character can say more than 5 words in a row uninterrupted.
4. Write a story where the main character is the exact opposite of you.
5. Write a poem from the perspective of your best friend from elementary school.
6. Write a script with ten characters, but each character is only allowed to talk to two others.
7. Write a story that would have been your favorite story at age nineteen.
8. Write a poem about being inside while outside, or vice versa.
9. Write the script to the best day of your life that still has yet to come.
10. Write a story based upon your least favorite song.
